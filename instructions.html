Instructions

  • Your goal in this game is to guess the secret number
  • score: you have 20 attempts to find the secret Number
  • High Score: the highest score you have got
  • Check button: reset the game, except for high score
  • there will be a feedback to your guess
    • Too High: your guess is too high
    • Too Low: your guess is too low
    • Almost There: you're so Close
